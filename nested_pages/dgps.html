Our Surveying Services

1. DGPS (Differential GPS) Survey

Improve GPS Accuracy with Differential Corrections

DGPS enhances standard GPS positioning by using a reference station to correct positional errors, providing accuracy up to 0.5m to 2m.

  • Ideal for mapping, navigation, and infrastructure planning
  • Real-time or post-processing correction options available
  • Suitable for hydrographic surveys, GIS mapping, and asset management

    • 2. GPS Static Survey

    High-Precision Geodetic Control Surveys

    Static GPS is a long-duration, stationary observation method used to establish geodetic control points with sub-centimeter accuracy.

  • Best for land deformation monitoring, cadastral surveys, and large-scale engineering projects
  • Dual-frequency GNSS receivers ensure high precision
  • Post-processed data for reliable and permanent reference points

    • 3. RTK (Real-Time Kinematic) Survey

    Instant, High-Accuracy Positioning for Dynamic Applications

    RTK provides centimeter-level accuracy in real time, making it perfect for applications requiring immediate, precise results..

  • Ideal for construction, road alignments, and precision farming
  • Real-time corrections via radio or internet (NTRIP)
  • Faster data collection with reliable accuracy
