What is GPR Survey?

Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) is a geophysical method that uses electromagnetic waves to detect objects, voids, and anomalies beneath the surface. It is a fast, efficient, and non-destructive solution for underground investigations.

  • Detects utilities, pipelines, voids, and buried structures
  • Works on concrete, soil, asphalt, and rock
  • Provides real-time imaging with high accuracy
